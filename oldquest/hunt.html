Scavenger Hunt

There may be an occasion (or two) that you find your team is stumped on a difficult clue. The scavenger hunt is a mechanism in which you can earn points to buy extra help from HQ.

On the night of the quest, this website will be updated to list a number of items that can be collected and brought to HQ for points. Points will vary depending upon the difficulty to acquire each item. You may collect these items and turn them in throughout the quest, at any time.

HQ reserves the right to change point values at their discretion.
