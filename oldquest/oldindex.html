The Great Key West Quest: Challengers Edition

The Challengers Edition of the Great Key West Quest is a bona fide favorite for many Key West residents, both young and old alike. It challenges you and your team to solve riddles, decipher clues, tackle physical challenges, and outwit your competitors, all in pursuit of the Quest Trophy. Veterans of the Great Key West Quest over the more than twenty years of Quests will tell you that there is nothing to compare to the fun and excitement of searching for the Quest Trophy, and the memories you will make will last a lifetime!
